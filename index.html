Space Up

This small game shows an image and its name in French and English. Hold the phone to your forehead. When you guess correctly, tilt the device down. To pass, tilt up. Try to get as many correct as you can!

Best experienced on a phone. Scan the QR code to open Space Up on your device.

Please rotate your device to landscape mode to play Space Up.
